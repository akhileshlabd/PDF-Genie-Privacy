Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

Welcome to PDF Genie. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

PDF Genie is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect personal identification information (PII) or store your documents on any external servers.

  • Local Files: The app requires access to your device storage to read and process PDF files. These files remain on your device.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous crash logs to improve app stability.

2. Permissions

To function correctly, the app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage/Media: To select, view, and save PDF documents.
  • Internet: (If applicable) For processing or ads.

3. Data Security

Since all processing is done locally on your device, your data is as secure as your phone itself. We do not share your files with third parties.
